From printed cards to immersive digital experiences.

It started with a simple realization: planning an event is stressful enough, but inviting people shouldn't be. Traditional paper cards were expensive, hard to update, and offered no real-time interaction.

We built Schedy to bridge the gap between elegant design and modern utility. We wanted couples, event planners, and hosts to have a platform where they could build stunning event websites in minutes—complete with RSVP tracking, live maps, and photo galleries.

Today, we're proud to be the trusted platform for thousands of weddings, birthdays, and corporate events worldwide, turning simple invites into lasting memories.
